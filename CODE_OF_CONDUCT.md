# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Discord Archiver to be a welcoming project for everyone. As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience regardless of age, background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Be respectful:**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be kind when giving feedback on issues or pull requests
- Accept constructive criticism graciously

**Be responsible:**
- Only use and promote this tool for legitimate personal archival purposes
- Do not encourage or help others violate Discord's Terms of Service
- Respect other users' privacy — do not share archives containing other people's data

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Sharing or requesting help with misuse of the tool (mass scraping, ToS violations)
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private discussion or contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and acted on as quickly as possible. Maintainers have the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban users who violate this code of conduct.
